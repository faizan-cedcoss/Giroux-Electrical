Journeyman Electrician Responsibilities

  • Perform electrical work in a neat, organized and timely manner
  • Assemble, install, test, and maintain electrical wiring, equipment, appliances, fixtures, etc.
  • Connect wires to circuit breakers, transformers, or other components
  • Order electrical supplies
  • Work safely on a job site

Journeyman Electrician Requirements

  • Must be articulate and communicate extremely well with other team members and customers
  • Valid Massachusetts Electrical License required
  • Valid Driver’s License with clean driving record and no restrictions
  • Must have your own transportation and hand tools
  • Highly organized, precise and detail-orientated
  • Must work well under company structure and be process-driven

Journeyman Electrician Benefits

  • Full-time employment (40 hours per week) with potential for overtime
  • Competitive wages (based on experience)
  • Health Insurance, Dental, Disability Insurance, 401(k), paid vacation & sick time, paid holidays
  • Company vehicle to use for Giroux Electrical Contractors
  • A growing team of positive, motivated and fun individuals who love what they do
  • Open-door policy with management and encouragement for new ideas from any and all staff to help improve our processes
  • A variety of residential, commercial and industrial projects
